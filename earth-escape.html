Earth Escape

A vertical-scrolling 2D platformer set on a crumbling planet

Projects Experience Certifications Bio Contact

Earth Escape is a vertical-scrolling 2D platformer that places players in a thrilling journey through a post-apocalyptic Earth. Navigate ever upwards, dodge hazards, and prepare your elevator speech just in case you encounter the CEO.

Key Features

  • Vertical progression with increasing difficulty
  • Procedurally generated platforms and enemies based on player progression
  • Game over and retry system with quick restarts

Development Skills Demonstrated

  • Unity 2D Physics: Jump mechanics, gravity, and collision logic
  • C# Programming: Platform and enemy spawn logic, death zones, and scrolling behavior
